Publications

Professor Nii Narku Quaynor's key publications focus on various aspects of internet development and governance, particularly in Africa. Notable works include "Building Trust in the Digital Economy," co-authored J. K. Asamoah, which explores strategies for fostering trust in digital economies with developing countries, emphasizing cybersecurity and policy frameworks. His publication "Internet Governance in Africa" examines the unique challenges and opportunities in shaping localized internet policies. In collaboration wit Abena E., Quaynor discusses the intersection of cybersecurity and digital inclusion in "Cybersecurity and Digital Inclusion" highlights the necessity of secure internet access for socio-economic development.
